TL;DR: I'm a total noob at web development, but I absolutely love it. Watch this space!

Still here? Great, you must want to know some more.

I’ve gone from being a desktop techy at an ad agency, to co-owning a tech support business; I’m a qualified mountain guide and I run a national hiking community website with the full gambit of ad spots, social media competitions, merchandise and good quality content – It’s fair to say that I am a jack of more trades than I am a master of.

That being said, through my IT support business I regularly assisted clients getting their domains registered and assisted with some DNS record editing. This, combined with the passion project that is Hiking South Africa, started giving me a familiarity with the web and all its bits and pieces.

The final push in committing to web development full-time, came during the 2020 COVID-19 lockdown. After 11 years in tech support, it was starting to feel stale - the focussed time during lockdown allowed me to kickstart my basic knowledge of web development.

In a nutshell: I enjoy making things that look good and work well and I enjoy helping people – building websites ticks all my boxes.
